Data Deletion Request

River Tag respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data.

If you would like to request the deletion of your personal data associated with River Tag, please contact us at:

youlikey@gmail.com

When submitting a request, please include sufficient information to help us identify your account and an appropriate and valid reason for the request.

Processing Your Request

Once your request is received, we will verify your identity and process the deletion in accordance with applicable laws and platform requirements.

Data deletion requests are processed free of charge and are available to users in all regions.

Some data may be retained if required to comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, ensure security, or enforce our terms and policies.
